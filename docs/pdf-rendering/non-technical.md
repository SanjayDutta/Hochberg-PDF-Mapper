# PDF Rendering

## Overview
The PDF Rendering feature shows the uploaded PDF inside the app so users can review pages and place fields without leaving the workspace.

## Purpose
Its purpose is to provide a smooth reading and navigation experience after upload. Instead of opening files in an external viewer, users can continue in one flow and directly work on the document.

## How it works
1. After upload starts, the app shows a loading state while it prepares the PDF for display.
2. Once ready, the upload section is replaced by the PDF view so the transition feels seamless.
3. The center area displays one main page at a time with controls for moving to previous or next pages.
4. A small page navigator on the right shows mini previews, and users can click any preview to jump directly to that page.
5. Zoom controls let users increase or decrease page size for better readability.
6. On smaller screens, side panels are hidden to keep the reading area focused and usable.

## User Benefits
Users can quickly move through long PDFs, jump to specific pages, and adjust zoom without switching tools. This improves speed, reduces confusion, and makes field-mapping work more accurate.
